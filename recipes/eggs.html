Oven Scrambled Eggs

Description

A new way to make scrambled eggs

Ingredients

½ cup butter or margarine, melted 24 eggs ¼ cup sour cream 2 ¼ teaspoons salt 2 ½ cups milk

Steps

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Pour melted butter into a 9x13-inch glass baking dish. Whisk together eggs and salt in a large bowl until well-blended. Gradually whisk in milk. Pour egg mixture into the buttered dish. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Stir egg mixture and continue to bake until eggs are set, 10 to 15 minutes more.
